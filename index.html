Cody’s Biography

I grew up in the small rural community of Postville, Iowa. With the moniker “Hometown of the World”, Postville provided me with exposure to many different cultures, languages, and experiences. I graduated Postville High School in the spring of 2012. After graduation, I decided to pursue my dream of becoming a Hawkeye at the University of Iowa. I am now a senior and am pursuing bachelors’ degrees in Finance and Business Analytics and Information Systems (BAIS).

In addition to my academic coursework, I’m completing a 15-month Data Analyst Internship at AEGON Asset Management in Cedar Rapids, Iowa. During my internship I’ve implemented new software that is responsible for reporting daily trades. My other duties involve completing daily and quarterly cash flow analyses, as well as troubleshooting inconsistent data for AEGON daily traders.

For fun, I love to watch and play sports. My favorite sports teams include: the Green Bay Packers, Cleveland Indians, Los Angeles Lakers, and Iowa Hawkeyes. I also enjoy boating with my friends and family on the Mississippi River.
